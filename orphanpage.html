History of the Orphanage

The Eau Vive Welcome Center :

• To promote and carry out social and humanitarian works in favor of orphaned and underprivileged children.

• To promote the creation of reception and support structures for disadvantaged groups and people in distress.

• To work for their social integration through the creation of schools, colleges, universities, businesses, and agricultural and livestock activities.

• To provide support for elderly people.

The orphanage Sainte Famille of Messamendongo :

• Year established: 2005

• Mission: To care for children in distress and abandoned children

• Founder & Director: Mrs. ZANG AYONG, wife of Mr. ELANDI MBALLA Créscence Paule

• Location: Odza Borne 10

• Landmark: 200 meters behind Neptune Messamendongo gas station

We place special emphasis on the spiritual and educational development of children who are still of school age.

The orphanage divin of mendong :

• Purpose: To offer an unforgettable moment of togetherness

• Activities included:

• Singing

• Dancing

• Counseling session

• Haircuts for boys by a professional barber

• Entertainment by Mouton Mouton (influencer & comedian)

Statistics About Eau vive

We currently have a total of 82 children, including 60 living on-site, distributed as follows:

• 53 children regularly enrolled in school
• 7 children who are not yet of school age
• 1 deaf-mute child enrolled in school
• 1 abandoned newborn
• 3 older girls in charge of cooking
