About Cameroon Orphan Help

Building trust between donors and real orphanages

Who We Are

Cameroon Orphan Help is a student-developed web platform created to help people identify real and recognized orphanages in Cameroon. Our goal is to ensure that every donation reaches children who truly need support.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect donors with real and verified orphanages by providing a reliable platform that ensures their gifts reach the right hands. For those unable to deliver in person, we collect and deliver donations on their behalf—ensuring transparency, accountability at every step.

Our Vision

To become a trusted reference platform where every orphaned child is supported with love, dignity, and opportunity—through a transparent and trustworthy bridge between generous donors and verified orphanages.

Our Core Values

  • Transparency

  • Accountability

  • Social Responsibility

  • Compassion
